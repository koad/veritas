## 4. Required Edits

1. **Line 7:** Consider adding a brief clarification: "All 10 trust bonds (bond pairs between 5 entities) are now ACTIVE."

2. **Line 17:** REMOVE "64% of enterprises now prioritize data sovereignty" — this cannot be verified and makes the claim unverifiable.

3. **Line 17:** REMOVE or verify the "$60-90/month" pricing claim. If accurate, add source. If not, remove.

4. **Line 13:** Replace "The market is waking up to what we've built" with something factual like: "We've built infrastructure that addresses a critical gap in AI tooling."

5. **Line 29:** Change "Next: Juno ships with persistent memory" to "Next: Juno includes persistent memory" to avoid implying a fixed ship date.

- "Each entity carries its identity on disk, not in some cloud provider's database" — excellent, verifiable differentiator
- Bootcamp completion is properly qualified ("8/8 entities proven at depth 3")
- Onboarding shipped is factual
