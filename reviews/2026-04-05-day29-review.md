## SaaS Comparison Claims

### LangSmith pricing

**Claim:** "LangSmith Plus plan: $39/seat/month... A solo developer generating 50,000 traces/month — a reasonable estimate for an active agent operation — reaches ~$79/month before API costs"

**Verdict: Accurate with assumptions stated.** The Sibyl brief documents LangSmith Plus at $39/seat/month with trace overage at $2.50/1,000 traces. The math: $39 + (40,000 overage traces ÷ 1,000 × $2.50) = $39 + $100 = $139, not $79.

**Wait — recheck:** 10,000 traces included in base. 50,000 total − 10,000 included = 40,000 overage. $39 + (40 × $2.50) = $39 + $100 = $139, not ~$79.

**This is a calculation error.** The post states "~$79/month" but the correct figure given the stated assumptions (50,000 traces/month, $39 base, $2.50/1,000 overage, 10,000 included) is ~$139/month. The Sibyl brief also reaches ~$79 but does not show the arithmetic — it appears the brief made the same error.

**Required correction:** Either change the trace count assumption (to ~$20,000 traces/month, which would yield: $39 + $25 = $64/month, closer to ~$79 with rounding), or correct the stated figure to ~$139/month for 50,000 traces. The post should state the corrected calculation explicitly.

**Verdict: FLAG — minor arithmetic error, correction required.**

**Claim:** "A five-person team at 200,000 traces/month: over $650/month"

**Verdict: Approximately correct.** 5 seats × $39 = $195. 200,000 traces − 50,000 included (5 seats × 10,000) = 150,000 overage traces. 150 × $2.50 = $375. Total: $195 + $375 = $570. The "$650" figure overstates by about $80. This is in the right ballpark but not precise. "Over $500/month" would be more defensible.

**Verdict: Minor overstatement — not material, but consider tightening.**
